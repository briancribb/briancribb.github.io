The folks at RunVermont asked me to get some video of this kids race, and of course I agreed. I guess I made myself into a resource after sharing all of my content from the Champ on Church Fun Run. Not that I’m complaining. A youth event is perfect for me as a spectator, since I don’t feel bad for not participating.

There are several events in the YAM Scram, but we couldn’t stay for the last one because we had plans to go to Montréal this weekend. I did get most of it, though. Have a look through the gallery, maybe you’ll see someone you know.
